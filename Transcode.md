
# Check balanced parens (@toomasv)

Sometimes you can also use the new transcode to check your strings for balanced parens:
>> transcode/trace %{a(b(c)d)e}% func [e i t l o][[open close] print [e mold i]]
open "(b(c)d)e"
open "(c)d)e"
close ")d)e"
close ")e"
== [a (b (c) d) e]
>> transcode/trace %{a(b((c)d)e}% func [e i t l o][[open close] print [e mold i]]
open "(b((c)d)e"
open "((c)d)e"
open "(c)d)e"
close ")d)e"
close ")e"
